We do not collect or share any personally identifiable information, such as your name, email, or phone number, with OpenAI.
  • 2. User Consent: By using the App, you consent to the processing of your image data by OpenAI in accordance with OpenAI's Privacy Policy https://openai.com/privacy. We ensure that the data is processed securely and in compliance with all applicable data protection laws.
  • 3. No Third-Party Use: We do not sell, rent, or lease your data to third parties. The data shared with OpenAI is used solely to improve the App's performance and provide plant identification features. OpenAI does not use your data for any other purposes outside the scope of providing these services.
